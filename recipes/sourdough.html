Sourdough

Description

Sourdough has been a staple food for many people for thousands of years. It is also really freaking delicious.

Sourdough is easy to make so long as you don't mind regularly checking in for a quick 2 minutes at the top of the hour for a half day.

Ingredients

Leaven

  • 30 grams sourdough starter
  • 75 grams whole wheat flour
  • 75 grams water

Dough

  • Leaven (see above)
  • 525 grams water
  • 700 grams whole wheat flour
  • 1 Tbsp salt

Directions

Mix it all up and fold
